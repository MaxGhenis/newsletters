We’re thrilled to share several major announcements from PolicyEngine US, reflecting our ongoing mission to make policy analysis more open, scientific, and accessible.


Enhanced Microdata and Tax Reform Analyses

We've launched the beta version of our enhanced microdata for US tax-benefit analysis, integrating IRS information and Congressional Budget Office forecasts into the Current Population Survey using cutting-edge data science techniques. This open dataset enables more accurate tax-benefit analysis than ever before.

Learn more about our methodology and explore the data.


Using our enhanced microdata, we analyzed the Child Tax Credit provisions in the Tax Relief for American Families and Workers Act (TRAFWA), projecting that, on average each year from 2023-2025, they would cost $13 billion, benefit 30% of Americans, and reduce child poverty by 7%. Read the full report and see how the TRAFWA CTC affects your own household with our personalized calculator.

Try our TRAFWA CTC Calculator

We also examined select individual income tax reforms in President Biden's 2025 budget proposal, estimating they would lower poverty by 12% and reduce income inequality in 2024. Dive into the analysis.
