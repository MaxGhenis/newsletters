## LinkedIn

**PolicyEngine's 2025 Year in Review**

2025 was a transformative year for PolicyEngine. Here's what we accomplished:

**United States**
- Expanded to 62 jurisdictions (federal + all 50 states + DC + localities)
- Served 100K+ people through API partners like MyFriendBen
- Analyzed every major OBBBA provision
- Launched the ACA Calculator, SALTernative, and GiveCalc
- Opened our DC office at Open Gov Hub
- Secured grants from Arnold Ventures and Pritzker Children's Initiative

**United Kingdom**
- Modeled all 650 constituencies and 300+ local authorities
- Analyzed all 9 Autumn Budget 2025 provisions
- Launched PolicyEngine 2.0 at Central Hall Westminster
- Received £250K Nuffield Foundation grant
- Released GiveCalc UK and the Local Areas Dashboard

**Open Source**
- 80 repositories, 26 contributors
- 3,800+ pull requests, 1,900+ code reviews
